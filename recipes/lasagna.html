Lasagna Recipe Description

This delicous recipe is for some nice thick lasagna noodles. This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. BONUS: itâ€™s make-ahead and freezer-friendly!

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • sausage
  • parsely
  • lasagna noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • tomato paste
  • ricotta cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • egg

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles
  2. Brown the meat
  3. Layer the noodles in a 13x9 pan alternating layers with the ricotta cheese, meat, and tomato sauce
  4. Top with mozzarella cheese
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes
